About

Co-founded in 2017 by Younes « CCL » Lazaar and Anas « Kefta » Houmaid, BRUSSELS CHALLENGE is a fighting games tournament series in Belgium. We aim to develop the fighting games scene through events and support to the community.

After a successful "BRUSSELS CHaLLENGE WINTER FIGHTS" The next step is "BRUSSELS CHALLENGE MAJOR EDITION" happening in april 2019. A lot of new features and events will be announced during the year.
